How to use

WORD Counter breaks down all the characters and words present in the text.
Density: You can chose to view the breakdown of the character usage by selecting the 'Character' radio button in the Density section at the top of the page, Or you can choose to view how often you use a particular word by selecting the 'Keyword' radio button.
Filter Out: Selecting 'Common Words' removes any occurence of common words most of them from the top 100 most common words in English Language [Wikipedia] found in the text. On the other hand, selecting 'None' won't filter anything.

WORD Counter only shows the top 10 density of words.
